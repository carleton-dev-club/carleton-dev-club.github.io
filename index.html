About Carleton .DevClub()

The Carleton dev club is a group dedicated to helping students to develop their coding skills, while pushing them to develop a portfolio, and to network with their peers.

The Carleton dev club meets every Monday, from 5:30 to 8:30 in HP4125. We will be hosting minor events, tech talks, and workshops periodically for students to learn new and exciting languages, and ways of applying their knowledge.
